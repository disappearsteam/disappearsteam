Why we're
here?

With strong security and the right technology, people and institutions today can still communicate securely and freely without worrying about someone looking over your should. In January 2016, when we looked at the available technology, we saw messaging apps that were insecure and that once you send a message or piece of data on the web it is out there forever.
We saw automated approaches that failed against adaptive adversaries, and all-or-nothing access controls that forced organizations to make unacceptable trade-offs between collaborating and securing sensitive data from misuse. We saw a need for a different kind of technology, and we knew it would take a different kind of company to build it. That’s why we founded Disappears.
